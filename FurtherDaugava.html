The towers of churches across the river keep watch over you while you walk along the shore.

"We need more people. Everyone needs more people working for them nowadays. When I talk to traders, they also tell me how there is a lack of people who know how to handle the goods.

Guess we can only hope for newcomers, but how long can we work this way?"
